A Few Words About Us

M.R.Sharma & Co. was established in the year 1989 as proprietary concern and converted into partnership firm w.e.f April 1, 2012.. We provide services in the field of Accounting, Auditing & Assurance, Internal Audit, Tax Audit, Management Audit, Statutory Audit, Income Tax, Tax Planning, Direct Taxes, Service Tax, Transfer Pricing, Maharashtra Value Added Tax, Company incorporation, Corporate Compliance, Formation and Management of Charitable Trusts and Formation and Compliance with Partnership Law, Valuation of Business and Secretarial services etc.

M.R Sharma & Co. is a professionally managed firm. The team consists of distinguished chartered accountants, Company secretaries, corporate financial advisors and tax consultants. The firm represents a combination of specialized skills, which are geared to offer sound financial advise and personalised proactive services. Those associated with the firm have regular interaction with industry and other professionals which enables the firm to keep pace with contemporary developments and to meet the needs of its clients.
